Capabilities

Everything happens
on your machine.

DictateMe runs a full speech-to-text pipeline locally. Your audio never leaves your computer unless you explicitly choose a cloud LLM.

🎤

Local Whisper STT

Powered by faster-whisper with CTranslate2. 4x faster than OpenAI Whisper with the same accuracy. GPU and CPU support.

🧠

Smart LLM Cleanup

Removes filler words, fixes grammar, adds punctuation. Uses Ollama locally or your preferred cloud provider — OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq.

🎨

Instant Reformatting

Dictate once, reformat as email, Slack message, bullet points, code comments, or any custom style. One keypress.

🌐

99 Languages

Auto-detects your language or pick one from the dropdown. Speak in English, German, Japanese, Albanian, or any of 99 Whisper-supported languages.

⌨

Keyboard-First

Press 1-8 to pick a format, Enter to insert, Escape to cancel. No mouse needed. Text goes straight to your cursor in any app.

🔒

Works Everywhere

System-wide insertion via clipboard or SendInput. Works in any app — VS Code, Outlook, Chrome, Slack, terminals, anywhere you type.

How It Works

Three seconds from
thought to text.

Hold the hotkey, speak naturally, release. DictateMe handles the rest.

1
🎤

Speak

Hold Ctrl+Shift+D and speak naturally. Voice Activity Detection captures only your speech, ignoring background noise.

2
⚡

Process

Whisper transcribes your speech locally. An LLM cleans up filler words, fixes grammar, and matches the tone to your context.

3
✏️

Insert

The polished text is instantly inserted wherever your cursor is. Your clipboard is preserved. Choose a format or let it auto-insert.

A floating overlay appears near your cursor during dictation. See your words as they're processed, pick a format, and it vanishes.

Connect Your LLM

Your brain,
your choice of AI.

DictateMe uses an LLM to clean up filler words and reformat text. Use a local model for full privacy, or connect a cloud provider for speed.

🤖

Local with Ollama

Install Ollama, run ollama pull llama3.2:3b, and you're set. Fully offline, fully private. No API key needed.

🔑

Cloud Providers

Paste your API key in Settings and pick a provider. Supports OpenAI (GPT-4o-mini), Anthropic (Claude), Groq (Llama), or any OpenAI-compatible endpoint.

⛔

Or Skip It Entirely

Don't want LLM cleanup? Turn it off in Settings. You'll get raw Whisper transcription — still great for most use cases, and transcription stays fully local either way.
